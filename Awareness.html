Namami Gange - Our Mission

Our Mission

The Namami Gange Programme is an integrated conservation mission, approved as a flagship programme by the Government of India, to accomplish the twin objectives of effective abatement of pollution and conservation and rejuvenation of the National River Ganga. Our mission is to ensure the ecological and cultural integrity of the Ganga River by fostering sustainable practices, community participation, and innovative solutions.

Our Core Values

  • Sustainability: Promoting environmentally sustainable practices to protect the Ganga for future generations.
  • Community Engagement: Encouraging active participation of local communities in conservation efforts.
  • Innovation: Leveraging cutting-edge technology and research to address pollution and ecological challenges.
  • Cultural Preservation: Respecting and preserving the spiritual and cultural significance of the Ganga.
  • Collaboration: Partnering with government, NGOs, and global organizations to achieve our goals.

Our Goals

  • Reduce pollution levels in the Ganga through effective sewage treatment and industrial effluent management.
  • Restore the ecological health of the river by enhancing biodiversity and maintaining river flow.
  • Promote sustainable agriculture and livelihoods along the Ganga basin.
  • Educate and engage communities to foster a sense of ownership and responsibility towards the river.
  • Develop and implement innovative solutions for long-term river conservation.
